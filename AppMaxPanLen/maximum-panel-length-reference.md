## 5. Noroc Panel Handling Note

Noroc panels are **no longer limited to 40 ft**.

A new handling equipment schedule planned for **October 2025** will allow handling Noroc panels up to **52 ft**.

### Important caveats

- Fewer panels may be stacked on a pallet.
- The installer must be properly equipped to lift and handle panels of this size and load.

> Operational warning: **Make sure the installer is geared to lift this kind of load.**

---

## 6. Norex-H — Horizontal Installation

When **Norex-H** panels are installed horizontally, the maximum length is constrained by **installation challenges**.

Contrary to **Norex-L**, the limiting variable for Norex-H is **not the colour of the panel**.

### Horizontal Norex-H limits

| Condition | Maximum |
|---|---:|
| Norex-H with a profile | Approximately **24 ft** |
| Norex-H with no profile | See the applicable **Maximum Panel Length Table** above |
| Maximum width | **41.5 in** |
